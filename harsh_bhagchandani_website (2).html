The Artist

Where Dance
Becomes a Lifestyle

Harsh Bhagchandani is one of India's most dynamic dance artists — a choreographer who doesn't just teach steps, but builds entire movement philosophies. Born and bred in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, his signature style "BollyHop" fuses the soul of Bollywood with the raw energy of Hip-Hop.

As Director of Feel The Vibe Studios, he leads a 12-member professional team delivering high-octane choreography for corporate shows, wedding sangeets, and large-scale stage productions — over 300 stage shows and 100+ family events to date.

His digital presence on Instagram (@harshbhagchandani_) and the Dance Marine YouTube channel has built a community of over 865,000 followers who tune in daily for trending tutorials, lifestyle content, and step-by-step breakdowns.
